About Niko Venag

Hi, my name is Niko Venag and I'm a Software Developer.

Since I was 15 years old I was always fascinated by programming and as everybody I wanted to build my own website. It always looked difficult to me since the age of 22 when I got involved in a course where I learnt the basic programming languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery etc). Since that moment I was hooked with how easy you can learn programming.

I was introduced to programming for a first time when I was studying Biotechnology degree in the Universtity of Vic(Spain). We had Python classes where we were writing some easy scripts using random DNA sequences for a research. I have passed this subject but it was really difficult for me to understand Python(Ubuntu). I never got back again to programming till the age of 22 when I signed for a programming course and my teacher answered all my questions and I was able to connect the dots. Since this moment I was hooked.

I have always believed that it's better to hear from somebody who is climbing the mountain than from somebody who already climbed it.

If you want to succeed as a Software Developer you have to learn and get smarter day after day. This will not be possibe if you are not disciplined and willing to put your time into this.

One of the most amazing things about being a programmer is that you can learn writing code without going to University. This doesn't mean that you will not have to study. But you can learn with your own speed.

My goal with this website is to motivate and inspire you to put down time to learn, grow and make more money.

As I'm very diverse person I will be sharing information about Cryptocurrency, Fitness, book reviews etc. My approach to life is not just to become a rich person but to become a fully developed in all aspect of life.
